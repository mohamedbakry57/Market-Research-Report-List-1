<p><strong>The Electrochemical Titrantors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Potentiometric Titrator</li><li>Coulometric Titrator</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrochemical titrators market encompasses various types of instruments used for quantitative chemical analysis. Potentiometric titrators measure the change in potential during a titration reaction to determine the analyte's concentration. Coulometric titrators utilize an electric current to generate a reagent in-situ, which reacts with the analyte for quantitative analysis. Other types of electrochemical titrators include amperometric titrators, which measure the current produced during a redox reaction for analysis. These instruments find applications in pharmaceutical, environmental, and chemical industries for accurate and efficient titration analysis.</p></p>
