<p><p>The Light Intensity Meter market primarily consists of two types: Normal Photodiode and Silicon Photodiode. Normal Photodiode is a commonly used type that measures light intensity using a semiconductor material. It is economical and suitable for general light detection applications. On the other hand, Silicon Photodiode features high sensitivity and accuracy due to the properties of silicon material. It is commonly used in scientific and precision measurement applications, where accurate and reliable light intensity measurements are crucial. Both types cater to different needs and applications in the light intensity meter market.</p></p>
