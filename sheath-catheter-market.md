<p><p>A sheath catheter, also known as an introducer sheath or vascular access sheath, is a medical device used to facilitate the insertion and removal of various interventional tools into the body during invasive procedures. It consists of a plastic or polymeric tube that acts as a conduit, allowing the passage of instruments while minimizing trauma to blood vessels.</p><p>The sheath catheter market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.20% during the forecast period. Several key factors drive this market growth. Firstly, the rising prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, such as coronary artery disease and congestive heart failure, is increasing the demand for cardiac catheterization procedures. Sheath catheters are often used in these procedures to improve access and maneuverability during interventions.</p><p>Additionally, the growing geriatric population, who are more susceptible to chronic diseases, is further propelling the market. The elderly tend to require more interventional procedures, such as angioplasty and stenting, which involve the use of sheath catheters.</p><p>Moreover, technological advancements in sheath catheter design and material composition are also contributing to market growth.
