<p><ul><li>Multi Layer</li><li>Single Layer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reflective polarizer film is a type of optical film that helps control the polarization of light. Multi-layer reflective polarizer films consist of multiple layers of materials with different refractive indices, which interact with incident light to produce a polarizing effect. On the other hand, single-layer reflective polarizer films use a single layer of material that acts as a polarizer. Both types of films are used in various applications including displays, automotive, and optical filters to enhance contrast, reduce glare, and improve visibility in electronic devices and other optical systems.</p></p>
