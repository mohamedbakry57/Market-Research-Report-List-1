<p><p>The pad printing supplies market consists of three main types: inks, pads, and plates. Inks are essential for transferring the desired image onto the product surface. They come in various colors and formulations to cater to different printing needs. Pads act as a medium between the inked plate and the product, picking up the ink and transferring it accurately. They are available in different shapes, sizes, and materials to accommodate various printing requirements. Plates serve as the carrier for the image to be printed and are often made of metal or polymer materials.</p></p>
