<p><p>Refrigeration coolers are used in various industries for cooling purposes. The market for refrigeration coolers can be categorized into different types based on the refrigerants used. These include HFC/HFO, NH3, CO2, Glycol, and others. HFC/HFO (Hydrofluorocarbon/Hydrofluoroolefin) coolers use synthetic refrigerants to cool the system. NH3 (Ammonia) coolers use ammonia, a natural refrigerant known for its high efficiency. CO2 (Carbon Dioxide) coolers use CO2 as a refrigerant, which is environmentally friendly. Glycol coolers use a mixture of water and ethylene or propylene glycol as a coolant. The "Others" category includes coolers using different refrigerants like hydrocarbons or a mix of other gases.</p></p>
