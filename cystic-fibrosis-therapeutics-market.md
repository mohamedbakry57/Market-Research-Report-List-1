<p><p>Cystic Fibrosis Therapeutics Market Types include treatments that target different aspects of the disease. PERT helps with digestion by providing enzymes. Anti-infectives target infections commonly associated with CF. Bronchodilators help relax and open the airways, making breathing easier. These different types of therapies work together to manage the symptoms of cystic fibrosis and improve the quality of life for patients.</p></p>
