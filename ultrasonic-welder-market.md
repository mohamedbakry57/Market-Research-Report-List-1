<p><p>Ultrasonic welders are categorized into two main types: plastic ultrasonic welders and metal ultrasonic welders. Plastic ultrasonic welders are used for joining thermoplastic materials together, such as in the automotive, packaging, and electronics industries. Metal ultrasonic welders, on the other hand, are utilized in industries that require the joining of metal components, like wires, terminals, and tubes. Both types of ultrasonic welders employ high-frequency vibrations to create friction and heat, which ultimately results in a strong and reliable bond between the materials being welded.</p></p>
