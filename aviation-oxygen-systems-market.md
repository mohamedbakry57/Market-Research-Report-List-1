<p><p>Aviation Oxygen Systems market includes two main types: Airborne Modular Oxygen System and Portable Liquid Oxygen System. The Airborne Modular Oxygen System provides oxygen supply to aircraft crew and passengers in a controlled cabin environment. It is installed inside the aircraft and offers various modes of oxygen delivery. On the other hand, the Portable Liquid Oxygen System is a compact, portable device that provides individual oxygen supply to pilots and crew during flight. It consists of a liquid oxygen reservoir and a breathing system, allowing for convenient use in emergency situations.</p></p>
