<p><p>Kiln furniture refers to heat-resistant supports or shelves used in kilns for firing ceramics. The market for kiln furniture is primarily divided into four main types: Silicon Carbide, Cordierite, Mullite, and Alumina. Silicon Carbide is known for its high thermal conductivity and strength. Cordierite is valued for its low thermal expansion and thermal shock resistance. Mullite offers excellent thermal stability and resistance to chemical attack. Alumina is preferred for its high temperature resistance and durability. Each type of kiln furniture offers unique benefits for specific firing applications.</p></p>
