<p><ul><li>Broadcast-Based Technologies</li><li>Network-based Technology</li><li>InterUSS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Remote Drone Identification System market encompasses three types: Broadcast-Based Technologies, Network-Based Technologies, and the InterUSS market. Broadcast-Based Technologies transmit identification signals from drones to receivers on the ground. Network-Based Technologies use internet connectivity to transmit drone identification data in real-time to authorized parties. The InterUSS market focuses on coordinating drone operations using a decentralized information exchange system. These types of systems play crucial roles in ensuring the safe and efficient integration of drones into airspace regulations and management.</p></p>
