<p><p>The Extended Warranty Agreement market includes two main types: the Standard Protection Plan and the Accidental Protection Plan. The Standard Protection Plan is designed to cover any defects or malfunctions that occur during regular use of a product, providing repairs or replacements as needed. On the other hand, the Accidental Protection Plan offers additional coverage for accidental damages such as spills, drops, or other unforeseen events that may cause harm to the product. These plans provide customers with peace of mind and ensure their investment is protected.</p></p>
