<p><p>The solar energy and battery storage market encompasses various types of batteries used for storing energy generated from solar sources. Lead-acid batteries have been widely used due to their low cost, but they have limited capacity and shorter lifespan. Li-ion batteries offer higher energy density, longer life, and faster charging, making them popular for both residential and commercial applications. Sodium-sulfur (NaS) batteries have large storage capacity and high efficiency, but they are expensive and require high operating temperatures. These market types differ in terms of cost, capacity, lifespan, and suitability for different applications.</p></p>
