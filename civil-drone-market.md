<p><p>A civil drone, also known as an unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV), is an aircraft operated without a human pilot on board. The increasing adoption of drones by government agencies for surveillance and law enforcement purposes is also contributing to the market expansion.</p><p>Overall, the civil drone market is poised for substantial growth in the coming years, driven by advancements in technology, increasing applications across various industries, and the need for cost-effective and efficient solutions.</p></p>

<p><p>The civil drone market comprises two main types: fixed wing drones and rotary wing drones. Fixed wing drones are similar to traditional planes, with a fixed wing and propellers, allowing them to fly long distances and stay airborne for extended periods. They are commonly used for mapping, surveillance, and agricultural purposes. On the other hand, rotary wing drones, also known as multirotor drones, are characterized by multiple rotors and a vertical takeoff and landing capability. These drones offer more maneuverability and are commonly used for aerial photography, surveillance, and delivery services.</p></p>
