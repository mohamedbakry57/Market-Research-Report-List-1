<p><p>POS systems for barbershops come in two market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based systems operate through internet servers, allowing users to access data and manage the system remotely. It offers flexibility, scalability, and real-time data updates. On the other hand, on-premises systems are physically installed on-site and managed locally. They offer more control, customization, and data privacy but require maintenance and upfront investment.
