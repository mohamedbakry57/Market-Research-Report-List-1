<p><strong>The Automotive IGBT Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CIB/PIM</li><li>IPM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive IGBT market can be classified into two types: CIB/PIM (Converter Inverter Brake/Power Integrated Module) and IPM (Intelligent Power Module). CIB/PIM modules combine the functions of converter, inverter, and brake systems into a single device, optimizing space and improving efficiency. On the other hand, IPM modules integrate advanced control and protection features along with power switches, offering higher performance and reliability. Both types cater to the growing demand for compact, efficient, and intelligent power solutions in the automotive industry.</p></p>
