<p><p>EUS-FNA (Endoscopic Ultrasound-Fine Needle Aspiration) is a technique used to sample tissues in the gastrointestinal tract for diagnosis. There are two market types for EUS-FNA: core puncture and edge puncture. Core puncture involves inserting a larger needle directly into the target tissue to obtain a larger tissue sample. On the other hand, edge puncture involves a smaller needle that grazes the tissue edge, collecting smaller tissue fragments. Both techniques aim at providing accurate tissue sampling for precise diagnoses in various gastrointestinal conditions.</p></p>
