These membranes consist of a perfluorinated polymer backbone with sulfonic acid groups, allowing for efficient proton transport while blocking the passage of gas or liquid. They are commonly used in applications requiring high performance, such as transportation and stationary power generation. Perfluorosulfonic Acid Membranes are a popular choice in the fuel cell industry for their reliability and durability.</p></p>
