<p><p>The Loop Parcel Sortation Systems market comprises three main types of sorters: Cross-Belt Sorters (Horizontal), Flat Sorters/Bomb Bay Sorters, and Tilt Tray Sorters. Cross-Belt Sorters use a continuous loop belt to transport parcels horizontally, allowing for precise and efficient sorting. Flat Sorters/Bomb Bay Sorters employ sliding trays to move parcels vertically and horizontally, enabling high-speed sorting. Tilt Tray Sorters use tilting trays to transport parcels, allowing for flexibility and gentle handling. These sortation systems are widely used in the logistics industry to streamline parcel sorting processes and improve operational efficiency.</p></p>
