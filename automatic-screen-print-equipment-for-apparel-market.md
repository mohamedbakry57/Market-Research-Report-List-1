<p><p>Automatic screen print equipment is widely used in the apparel market for efficient printing processes. In-line systems involve a continuous production flow where garments are fed into the printer and printed simultaneously, reducing labor and time. Carousel systems, on the other hand, involve a rotating set of stations that print one color at a time, allowing for multi-color designs. Both systems offer high-speed printing, precise placement, and consistency. They cater to the needs of apparel manufacturers by offering streamlined production, quality prints, and increased productivity.</p></p>
