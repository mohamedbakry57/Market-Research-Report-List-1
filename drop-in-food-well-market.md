<p><p>Drop-in food wells are used in commercial food service establishments to hold and display pre-packaged or pre-cooked food. There are three types of drop-in food wells: refrigerated, hot, and dual temperature. Refrigerated drop-in food wells maintain a cool temperature to keep perishable items like salads or desserts fresh. Hot drop-in food wells keep food warm for extended periods and are ideal for items like soups or cooked entrees. Dual temperature food wells have separate compartments for both hot and cold items, making them versatile for different food types.</p></p>
