These devices allow patients to manage their conditions at home and reduce hospital visits. Secondly, advancements in technology, such as wearable devices and smartphone applications, have made remote patient monitoring more accessible and user-friendly. Additionally, the rising aging population and the growing preference for home healthcare services are further driving the market growth.</p><p>The market for remote patient monitoring devices is also witnessing various trends.
