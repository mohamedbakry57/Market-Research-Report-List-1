DLaTGS detectors are known for their high sensitivity and low noise properties, making them ideal for applications like environmental monitoring and infrared spectroscopy. LiTaO3 detectors offer excellent temperature stability and fast response times, making them suitable for gas analysis and non-contact temperature measurement. PZT detectors provide a wide dynamic range and are commonly used in motion detection systems and security applications. Overall, each type of pyroelectric detector serves specific purposes based on their unique characteristics.</p></p>
