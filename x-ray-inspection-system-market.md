<p><ul><li>Film-Based Imaging</li><li>Digital Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>X-ray Inspection System Market consists of two main types - Film-Based Imaging and Digital Imaging. Film-Based Imaging involves the use of X-ray film to capture images, which are then developed and analyzed visually. On the other hand, Digital Imaging utilizes digital detectors to capture X-ray images, which can be viewed and analyzed on electronic devices. The Digital Imaging market is growing rapidly due to its advantages such as faster processing, easier storage and sharing of images, and enhanced image quality compared to Film-Based Imaging.</p></p>
