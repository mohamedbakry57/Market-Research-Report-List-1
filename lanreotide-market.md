It is primarily used in the treatment of neuroendocrine tumors (NETs), specifically those originating from the gastrointestinal tract and pancreas. Lanreotide works by binding to somatostatin receptors on these tumors, thereby reducing the production and secretion of hormones that contribute to tumor growth.</p><p>The Lanreotide Market has been experiencing significant growth and is expected to continue growing at a CAGR of 14.8% during the forecast period.
