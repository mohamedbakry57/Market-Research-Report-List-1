<p><p>The Roller Hearth Kiln Market encompasses two main types: Continuous Kiln and Batch Kiln. Continuous Kiln refers to a process where materials continuously move through the kiln on a roller conveyor, allowing for continuous production and high efficiency. On the other hand, Batch Kiln involves a batch-wise processing approach, where materials are loaded and unloaded in batches, resulting in a slower production rate but providing greater flexibility for different product types and variations. Both market types have their advantages and are commonly used in various industries, depending on the specific requirements and scale of production.</p></p>
