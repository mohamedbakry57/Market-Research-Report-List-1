<p><strong>The Cooking Oil Testers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Contactless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cooking oil testers can be classified into two types: contact testers and contactless testers. Contact testers require the oil sample to be in direct physical contact with the testing device, typically through dipping or immersing the tester into the oil. These testers analyze the oil's composition and quality through physical contact. On the other hand, contactless testers use advanced technologies like infrared spectroscopy or electronic sensors to analyze the oil without any physical contact. These testers provide accurate results without the need for direct interaction with the oil sample.</p></p>
