<p><p>The cutting disk market can be classified into two types: smooth edge and jagged edge. Smooth edge cutting disks are designed to create clean and precise cuts, making them suitable for applications that require smooth and polished edges, such as glass cutting. On the other hand, jagged edge cutting disks are intended for more aggressive cutting tasks, as they have teeth or serrations along the edge, allowing them to quickly cut through tough materials like metal or concrete. Ultimately, the choice between the two types depends on the specific cutting needs of different industries and applications.</p></p>
