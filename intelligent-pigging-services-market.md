<p><p>Intelligent Pigging Services refers to the inspection of pipelines using specialized devices called pigs. There are two main types of Intelligent Pigging Services: Magnetic Flux Leakage (MFL) and Ultrasonic Test (UT). MFL technology uses magnetic fields and sensors to detect and measure any corrosion or defects on the pipeline walls. UT technology, on the other hand, utilizes ultrasonic waves to identify cracks, metal loss, or other abnormalities. Both MFL and UT play a crucial role in ensuring the integrity and safety of pipelines by providing accurate and detailed inspection data.</p></p>
