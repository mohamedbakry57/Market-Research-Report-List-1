<p><p>The logo design service market consists of various types of logos. Monogram Logos or Lettermarks are made up of initials or letters. Wordmarks or Logotypes are logo designs based on the name of the company or brand. Pictorial Marks or Logo Symbols use visual representations to convey the brand's message or identity. Abstract Logo Marks are non-representational designs with unique shapes and forms. Mascots are character-based logos. Combination Marks combine text and symbol in a single design. Emblems are logos that consist of text enclosed within a symbol or icon.</p></p>
