<p><p>The yacht anchor market is primarily divided into five types: plow yacht anchors, flat yacht anchors, grapnel yacht anchors, floating yacht anchors, and other anchors. </p><p>Plow yacht anchors are known for their strong holding power in various seabeds. Flat yacht anchors are lightweight and easy to stow, making them popular for small yachts. Grapnel yacht anchors have multiple arms and are suitable for rocky or weedy bottoms. Floating yacht anchors are designed to keep the yacht in position without sinking. Lastly, the "other" category encompasses various anchor types that do not fit into the aforementioned categories, offering different functionalities depending on specific yacht requirements.</p></p>
