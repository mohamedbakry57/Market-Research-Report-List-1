<p><p>Industrial interlock switches are safety devices used in industrial settings to ensure that equipment or machinery is operated safely. These switches are designed to function as a safeguard, preventing machines from being operated when certain safety conditions are not met. They typically consist of two parts – an actuator and a switch – that are connected to each other in such a way that the actuator must be engaged in order for the switch to close. This ensures that the machine can only be operated when the actuator is in the correct position, indicating that all necessary safety measures have been taken.</p><p>The future outlook of the industrial interlock switches market is promising, with a forecasted CAGR of 12.1% during the forecasted period.
