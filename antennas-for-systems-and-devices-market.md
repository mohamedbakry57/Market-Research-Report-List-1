<p><p>The market for antennas in systems and devices can be categorized into three types: metal, ceramic, and PTFE. Metal antennas are made of metallic materials and are widely used due to their cost-effectiveness and good performance. Ceramic antennas are composed of ceramic materials and are known for their durability and high-temperature resistance. PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene) antennas are made from a synthetic fluoropolymer and offer excellent electrical insulation and high-frequency performance. Each antenna type has its unique properties and applications, catering to the diverse needs of various systems and devices.</p></p>
