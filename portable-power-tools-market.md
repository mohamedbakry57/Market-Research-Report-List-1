<p><p>Portable power tools market is categorized into two main types: corded tools and cordless tools. Corded tools are powered by electricity and require a constant connection to a power source through a cord. They provide a continuous and reliable power supply, allowing longer periods of use. On the other hand, cordless tools are battery-operated and offer greater mobility and flexibility, as they do not require a direct power source. Although they have limited run-time due to battery life, advancements in battery technology have improved their performance and increased their popularity in recent years.</p></p>
