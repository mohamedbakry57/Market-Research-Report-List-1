<p><p>Static Light Scattering (SLS) is a technique used in the field of analytical chemistry to measure the size, molecular weight, and structure of macromolecules such as proteins, polymers, and nanoparticles. It involves shining a beam of light onto a sample and measuring the intensity of the scattered light at various angles. This information is then used to calculate the particle size distribution and other relevant parameters.</p><p>The Static Light Scattering (SLS) Market is expected to witness substantial growth in the coming years. The increasing demand for advanced analytical techniques and instruments in the pharmaceutical and biotechnology industries is one of the key drivers of market growth. SLS is widely used in these industries for characterization and quality control of biopharmaceuticals, drug delivery systems, and nanomedicines. Additionally, the growing focus on research and development activities in the areas of material science and nanotechnology is also contributing to the market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, the introduction of technologically advanced SLS instruments is expected to propel the market forward.
