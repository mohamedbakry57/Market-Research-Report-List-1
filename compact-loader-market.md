<p><p>The compact loader market consists of four main types of loaders: backhoe loaders, wheeled loaders, compact track loaders, and skid steer loaders. Backhoe loaders are versatile machines with a loader on the front and a digging arm at the back. Wheeled loaders are equipped with large wheels for maneuverability on various terrains. Compact track loaders have tracks instead of wheels, offering enhanced traction in challenging conditions. Skid steer loaders are compact and agile, known for their ability to turn on the spot. These loaders cater to different needs in construction, agriculture, and landscaping industries.</p></p>
