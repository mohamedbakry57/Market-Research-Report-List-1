<p><ul><li>OEM</li><li>Aftermarket</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Armrest market is divided into two types: OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) and Aftermarket. OEM armrests are produced by the original car manufacturer and are included in the vehicle at the time of production. They are specifically designed for each specific car model, offering a seamless integration and often higher quality. On the other hand, Aftermarket armrests are produced by third-party manufacturers and can be purchased and installed by car owners after purchasing the vehicle. Aftermarket armrests provide more customization options and compatibility with different car models.</p></p>
