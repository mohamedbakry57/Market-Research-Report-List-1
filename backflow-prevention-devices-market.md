<p><p>Backflow prevention devices are used to prevent the reverse flow of contaminated or potentially hazardous substances into a clean water supply. There are different types of backflow prevention devices available in the market. Check valves are basic devices that allow liquid to flow in one direction only, preventing backflow. Reduced pressure zone devices create a barrier between the water supply and potentially contaminated water. Atmospheric vacuum breakers prevent back-siphoning by allowing air into the system. Pressure vacuum breakers have a similar function but are more complex. Other types of backflow prevention devices may include double check valves and spill-resistant pressure vacuum breakers.</p></p>
