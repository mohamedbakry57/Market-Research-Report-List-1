<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The HVAC Air Handling Units market can be categorized into two types: Draw Through Type and Blow Through Type. In Draw Through Type units, the fan is positioned before the filter and cooling coil, resulting in the air being drawn through the components. On the other hand, Blow Through Type units have the fan positioned after the filter and cooling coil, resulting in the air being blown through the components. Both types have their own advantages and are commonly used in different HVAC applications based on specific requirements and design considerations.</p></p>
