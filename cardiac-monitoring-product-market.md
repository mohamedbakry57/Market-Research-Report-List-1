<p><p>The cardiac monitoring product market consists of various types, including mobile cardiac telemetry, event monitoring, Holter monitoring, extended Holter monitoring, and others. Mobile cardiac telemetry involves the use of a small device that continuously records the heart's electrical activity for an extended period. Event monitoring includes devices that are activated by the patient during specific episodes of symptoms. Holter monitoring uses a wearable device to record the heart's activity for 24 to 48 hours. Extended Holter monitoring is similar to Holter monitoring but extends the recording period. Other types may include implantable loop recorders or remote cardiac monitoring systems.</p></p>
