<p><p>Electro hydraulic servo valves are used to control fluid flow in various applications. Nozzle flapper valves utilize a nozzle and a flapper to regulate flow by varying the orifice size. Jet action valves direct the jet of fluid to change its direction, controlling the flow. Direct drive valves directly actuate the spool to control fluid flow. These types of valves are commonly used in industries like aerospace, oil and gas, and manufacturing where precision control of hydraulic systems is required for efficient operations.</p></p>
