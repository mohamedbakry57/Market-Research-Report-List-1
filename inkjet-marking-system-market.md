<p><ul><li>CIJ</li><li>DOD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Inkjet Marking System Market is comprised of two main types: Continuous Inkjet (CIJ) and Drop on Demand (DOD). CIJ systems use a continuous stream of ink droplets that are electrostatically charged and deflected to form characters on a substrate. DOD systems, on the other hand, selectively form characters by releasing precise droplets of ink onto the substrate. Both systems are widely used for product marking and coding applications in various industries, providing efficient, high-quality, and customizable printing solutions.</p></p>
