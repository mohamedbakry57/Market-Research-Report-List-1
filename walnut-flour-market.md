<p><p>The walnut flour market is divided into two main types: organic walnut flour and conventional walnut flour. Organic walnut flour is produced from walnuts that are grown without the use of synthetic pesticides, fertilizers, or genetically modified organisms. It is considered healthier and more environmentally friendly. On the other hand, conventional walnut flour is made from walnuts that may be treated with synthetic chemicals during cultivation. It is more widely available and less expensive compared to its organic counterpart.</p></p>
