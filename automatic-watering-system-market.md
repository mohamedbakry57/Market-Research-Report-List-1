<p><ul><li>Computer Operated</li><li>Timer Operated</li><li>Sensor Operated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic watering system market offers three main types of systems: computer operated, timer operated, and sensor operated. Computer operated systems are controlled through a computer program, allowing users to set specific watering schedules and customize irrigation settings. Timer operated systems use a pre-set timer to activate watering at specific intervals, providing basic automation. Sensor operated systems utilize sensors that detect moisture levels in the soil, triggering watering only when necessary, optimizing water usage and plant health. These systems cater to different user needs and preferences in automating their watering processes.</p></p>
