<p><p>The metal plating and finishing market encompasses various types such as platings, coatings, polishing, and others. Platings involve the electrochemical process of depositing a thin layer of metal onto a surface to enhance its properties. Coatings refer to the application of a protective layer on the metal surface to prevent corrosion or improve aesthetics. Polishing involves the mechanical process of smoothing a metal surface to create a reflective finish.
