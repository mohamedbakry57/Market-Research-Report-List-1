<p><p>The Electric Suction Apparatus market includes two main types: Single Tank Suction Apparatus and Double Tank Suction Apparatus. Single Tank Suction Apparatuses consist of a single canister or tank to collect the suctioned fluids, making them cost-effective and easy to maintain. On the other hand, Double Tank Suction Apparatuses have two separate canisters, allowing continuous suction by alternating between the two tanks. This type is commonly used in medical settings where a constant and uninterrupted suction supply is required. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
