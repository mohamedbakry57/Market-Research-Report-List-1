<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Electrical SCADA Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Remote Terminal Unit (RTU)</li><li>Master Terminal Unit (MTU)</li><li>Human Machine Interface (HMI)</li><li>Programmable Logic Controller (PLC)</li><li>Communication System</li><li>Others (Includes IED’s, Circuit relays, sensors etc.)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electrical SCADA market includes various types of systems used in monitoring and controlling electrical infrastructures. Remote Terminal Units (RTUs) are devices that gather data from sensors and send it to a central location. Master Terminal Units (MTUs) collect information from RTUs and manage the overall system. Human Machine Interfaces (HMIs) provide a visual interface for operators to interact with the SCADA system. Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs) automate processes based on received data. Communication Systems enable the exchange of information between different components. Other devices include IEDs, circuit relays, and sensors used for specialized functions.</p></p>
