<p><p>Flex Plastic IV Bags market consists of two main types: PVC Soft Bag and Non-PVC Composite Film Soft Bag. PVC Soft Bags are made from polyvinyl chloride and provide good gas barrier properties and transparency. However, concerns about plasticizer migration have led to the development of Non-PVC Composite Film Soft Bags. These bags are composed of multiple layers, such as polyolefins and ethylene-vinyl acetate, offering reduced plasticizer leaching and improved compatibility with a wide range of medications. Both types of bags are used for intravenous administration of fluids and drugs in healthcare facilities.</p></p>
