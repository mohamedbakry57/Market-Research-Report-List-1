<p><p>UHT processing involves four main market types. Heaters are used for rapidly heating the product to a high temperature. Homogenizers are employed to break down the fat globules and create a uniform texture. Flash cooling is then done to quickly reduce the temperature. Lastly, aseptic packaging is used to maintain the product's quality and shelf life by ensuring it is sealed in a sterile environment. These market types collectively contribute to the UHT processing industry, enabling the production of long-lasting, high-quality, and safe food and beverage products.</p></p>
