<p><p>The non-stick frying pans market is divided into two primary types: PTFE-based non-stick pans and ceramic-based non-stick pans. PTFE-based pans use a coating made from a synthetic polymer called polytetrafluoroethylene, which provides excellent non-stick properties and heat resistance. On the other hand, ceramic-based pans use a ceramic coating that is derived from inorganic materials, offering a natural non-stick surface. Both types of pans have gained popularity among consumers due to their ability to reduce the need for excessive oil or butter while cooking and ease of cleaning.</p></p>
