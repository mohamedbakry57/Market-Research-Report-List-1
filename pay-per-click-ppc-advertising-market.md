<p><ul><li>Flat-rate PPC</li><li>Bid-based PPC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pay-per-click (PPC) advertising has two main market types: flat-rate PPC and bid-based PPC. In the flat-rate PPC market, advertisers pay a fixed rate for each click, regardless of competition or keyword popularity. This model offers simplicity and predictability. On the other hand, the bid-based PPC market involves advertisers bidding on keywords, with the highest bidder enjoying better ad placement and increased visibility. This model allows for more flexibility and potential for higher ad exposure, but also introduces competition and cost uncertainties for advertisers.</p></p>
