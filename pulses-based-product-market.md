Pulse starches are extracted from pulses and used as thickeners or stabilizers in food processing. Pulse protein refers to the protein content extracted from pulses, often used as a plant-based protein source in food products. Pulse fiber is the dietary fiber derived from pulses, consumed for its health benefits. Pulse grits are coarsely ground pulses used in cooking or as a base for dishes.</p></p>
