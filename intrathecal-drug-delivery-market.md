<p><strong>The Intrathecal Drug Delivery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Constant Rate Pump</li><li>Programmable Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Intrathecal drug delivery systems involve two main types of pumps: constant rate pumps and programmable pumps. Constant rate pumps deliver a continuous and consistent flow of medication to the intrathecal space of the spinal cord. Programmable pumps, on the other hand, allow for more personalized and adjustable dosing schedules based on individual patient needs. Both types of pumps are used to deliver pain medication or other therapeutic agents directly to the spinal cord, providing targeted relief for chronic pain or spasticity.</p></p>
