<p><p>The column spot welder market can be categorized into two types: single-phase and three-phase. Single-phase spot welders operate on a single alternating current (AC) waveform and are generally used in domestic or light-duty industrial applications. On the other hand, three-phase spot welders work on a three-phase AC power supply and are suitable for heavy-duty industrial use, as they offer greater power and efficiency. These two types cater to different needs and requirements, providing options for various users and industries.</p></p>
