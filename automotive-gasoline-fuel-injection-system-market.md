<p><strong>The Automotive Gasoline Fuel Injection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Point or Throttle Body Injection</li><li>Port or Multipoint Fuel Injection</li><li>Sequential Fuel Injection</li><li>Direct Injection</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single-point or Throttle Body Injection system delivers fuel to the engine through a single point, usually located in the throttle body. Port or Multipoint Fuel Injection system has individual fuel injectors for each cylinder, providing more precise fuel delivery. Sequential Fuel Injection system delivers fuel to each cylinder in a specific order, improving fuel efficiency and performance. Direct Injection system directly injects fuel into the combustion chamber, resulting in better fuel efficiency. Other types of Fuel Injection systems include continuous or batch injection systems.</p></p>
