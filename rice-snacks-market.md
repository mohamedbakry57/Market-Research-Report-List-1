<p><p>The Rice Snacks market can be classified into two main types: Glutinous Rice Base and Non-Glutinous Rice Base. Glutinous Rice Base snacks are made from a sticky rice variety that contains high levels of amylopectin starch, resulting in a chewy and sticky texture. These snacks are commonly found in Asian cuisine and can be sweet or savory. On the other hand, Non-Glutinous Rice Base snacks are made from regular rice varieties that do not contain amylopectin starch. These snacks have a lighter and crispier texture and are commonly seen in various global cuisines.</p></p>
