The Diffraction Grating Spectrometer uses a diffraction grating to separate light, enabling precise wavelength analysis. The Interference Spectrometer employs an interferometer to measure the phase shift caused by light interference. Each type provides unique advantages in terms of accuracy, resolution, and wavelength range, catering to specific applications in industries like metallurgy, environmental testing, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
