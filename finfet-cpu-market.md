<p><p>A FinFET CPU, also known as a Fin Field-Effect Transistor CPU, is a type of CPU design that utilizes a three-dimensional transistor structure. This design provides superior performance and energy efficiency compared to traditional planar transistors.</p><p>The current outlook of the FinFET CPU market is highly positive.
