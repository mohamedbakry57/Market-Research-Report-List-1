<p><p>Parasitology Identification market includes various methods used to identify parasites. Microscopic identification involves examining samples under a microscope to identify the parasites based on their morphological characteristics. Molecular techniques use DNA analysis to detect and identify parasites accurately. Immunological techniques rely on the immune system's response to identify parasites through antibody-antigen reactions. Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDTs) provide quick and convenient identification using specific antibodies or antigens, often in the form of test strips. These different types of identification methods cater to different needs and preferences in parasitology research and diagnostics.</p></p>
