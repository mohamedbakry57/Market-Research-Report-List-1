<p><p>The multi-beam antenna market comprises various types, including multi-beam lens antenna, multi-beam reflector antenna, multi-beam phased array antenna, and others. Multi-beam lens antenna uses multiple lens systems to provide coverage across different service areas. Multi-beam reflector antenna employs a reflector to generate multiple beams and achieve a wide coverage range. Multi-beam phased array antenna utilizes an array of radiating elements with phased signals to form beams in different directions. Other types in the market include antennas that utilize complex geometric structures or combinations of different technologies to generate multiple beams.</p></p>
