<p><p>The encapsulated salt market encompasses three main types: solar salt, rock salt, and vacuum salt. Solar salt is produced by evaporating seawater in large ponds, resulting in high levels of purity. Rock salt is mined from underground deposits and contains impurities due to its natural formation process. Vacuum salt is obtained through an industrial process that involves evaporating brine under vacuum conditions, resulting in very pure salt crystals. Each type of salt offers distinct characteristics and uses, catering to different industries and consumer preferences.</p></p>
