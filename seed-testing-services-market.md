<p><p>Seed testing services market types include purity test, water test, dynamic test, germination test, and other market types. </p><p>Purity tests determine the presence of impurities or foreign matter in the seeds. Water tests measure the moisture content of seeds, which is crucial for determining seed quality and viability. Dynamic tests evaluate the physical strength and vigor of seeds, assessing their ability to withstand various conditions. Germination tests determine the percentage of seeds that are capable of producing healthy seedlings. The other market types may include tests for seed health, seedling growth, or genetic testing to identify specific traits in seeds.</p></p>
