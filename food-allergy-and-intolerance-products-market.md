<p><p>The market for food allergy and intolerance products can be divided into two main types. The first type is Immunoglobulin E (IgE) mediated food allergy, which occurs when the immune system overreacts to certain proteins in food, leading to allergic reactions. The second type is non-IgE mediated food allergy, where the immune system reacts differently, triggering symptoms such as gastrointestinal issues instead of traditional allergic reactions. Both types of food allergies require specialized products to manage and avoid triggering allergic reactions.</p></p>
