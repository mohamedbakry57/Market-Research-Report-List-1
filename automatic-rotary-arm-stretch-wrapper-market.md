<p><p>The Automatic Rotary Arm Stretch Wrapper market consists of two main types: Semi-Automatic and Fully-Automatic. Semi-Automatic stretch wrappers require some manual intervention in the wrapping process, such as attaching the film to the load and initiating the wrapping cycle. On the other hand, Fully-Automatic stretch wrappers are designed to operate without any manual assistance, as they automatically attach the film and initiate the wrapping cycle. Both types offer efficient and reliable solutions for wrapping palletized loads, with the fully-automatic option providing a higher level of automation and convenience.</p></p>
