<p><strong>The Mass Flow Controllers (MFC) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Type</li><li>Indirect Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mass flow controllers (MFC) are devices used to measure and control the flow rate of gases in various industries. Direct type MFCs have a built-in pressure sensor that directly measures the flow rate, providing accurate readings. Indirect type MFCs, on the other hand, use temperature sensors to indirectly calculate the flow rate based on thermal properties. While both types serve the same purpose, direct type MFCs are generally considered more accurate and reliable, while indirect type MFCs are often more cost-effective and suitable for certain applications.</p></p>
