<p><strong>The Flat Metal Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Veneer Type</li><li>Segmented Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flat metal detectors are widely used in various industries for metal detection purposes. There are two main types of flat metal detectors available in the market. The first type is the veneer type, which is made of a thin layer of metal integrated into a non-metallic substrate. This type ensures high detection sensitivity, making it suitable for industries such as food processing. The second type is the segmented type, which consists of multiple segments arranged in a grid pattern. This type provides accurate detection results, especially for larger objects, and finds applications in security screening and industrial manufacturing.</p></p>
