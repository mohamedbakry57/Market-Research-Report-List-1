<p><p>The wireless headset for gaming market can be classified into three types: Infrared, M Wave, and Decimeter Wave. Infrared headsets use infrared technology to transmit audio signals, typically with a limited range and line-of-sight requirements. M Wave headsets utilize magnetic waves for signal transmission, enabling a longer range and better penetration through obstacles. Decimeter Wave headsets employ higher frequency waves, allowing for even longer range and improved signal quality. Each type has its own advantages and limitations, catering to different gaming preferences and environments.</p></p>
