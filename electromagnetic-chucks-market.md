<p><p>Electromagnetic chucks are devices used to hold and secure ferromagnetic materials during various industrial processes. Rectangular electromagnetic chucks are designed in a rectangular shape and are suitable for holding workpieces with straight edges, while round electromagnetic chucks are circular in shape and are mainly used for securing cylindrical objects. Both types operate on the principle of electromagnetism, where an electric current creates a magnetic field to attract and hold the material. These chucks are widely used in manufacturing and machining industries for precise and efficient workpiece holding.</p></p>
