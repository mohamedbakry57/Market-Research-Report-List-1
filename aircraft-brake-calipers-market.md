<p><p>Aircraft brake calipers come in three primary types - Single Piston Brake Caliper Type, Double Piston Brake Caliper Type, and Multiple Piston Brake Caliper Type. Single piston brake calipers have a single piston pushing the brake pad against the rotor. Double piston brake calipers have two pistons applying pressure on either side of the rotor. Multiple piston brake calipers have more than two pistons for increased braking power and control. Each type offers unique benefits in terms of performance, reliability, and braking efficiency in aircraft systems.</p></p>
