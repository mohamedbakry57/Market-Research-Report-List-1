<p><strong>The IC Substrate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FC-BGA</li><li>FC-CSP</li><li>WB BGA</li><li>WB CSP</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>IC substrate market types include FC-BGA (Flip-Chip Ball Grid Array), FC-CSP (Flip-Chip Chip Scale Package), WB BGA (Wire Bonding Ball Grid Array), WB CSP (Wire Bonding Chip Scale Package), and Others. FC-BGA is a packaging technology that uses a flip-chip approach, where the die is flipped over and connected to the substrate using solder balls. FC-CSP is similar but with a smaller footprint. WB BGA uses wire bonding to connect the die to the substrate, while WB CSP is a smaller version of WB BGA. "Others" refers to any other IC substrate market types not listed above.</p></p>
