<p><p>Inkjet heads ejection technology refers to the process used to deliver ink droplets onto a surface for printing purposes. The market types include continuous inkjet heads and drop on demand inkjet heads. Continuous inkjet heads continuously emit tiny droplets of ink, whether they are needed or not, resulting in high-speed printing. Drop on demand inkjet heads, on the other hand, only eject ink droplets when required, leading to more precise and efficient printing. These market types cater to different printing needs and technological preferences.</p></p>
