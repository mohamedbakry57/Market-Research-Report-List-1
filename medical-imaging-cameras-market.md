<p><p>Medical imaging cameras are advanced imaging devices used in the medical field to capture and visualize internal body structures for diagnostic and therapeutic purposes. For instance, the integration of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning algorithms has improved image analysis and interpretation, enabling accurate and faster diagnoses.</p><p>The market is also witnessing a shift towards portable and handheld medical imaging cameras, allowing healthcare providers to capture images directly at the point-of-care. The increased focus on telemedicine and remote patient monitoring has also spurred the adoption of medical imaging cameras.</p><p>Overall, the medical imaging cameras market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 6% during the forecast period, driven by technological advancements, increasing healthcare expenditure, and the need for accurate and efficient diagnosis in the medical field.</p></p>
