<p><p>Prescriptive security market types can be categorized as either hosted or on-premise solutions. Hosted security involves outsourcing security functions to a third-party provider, who manages and secures the organization's infrastructure remotely. On the other hand, on-premise security involves implementing security measures within the organization's own infrastructure and managing it internally. Hosted solutions offer ease of use, cost savings, and scalability, while on-premise solutions provide more control and customization. Each market type has its advantages and organizations must choose the one that aligns with their specific security needs and preferences.</p></p>
