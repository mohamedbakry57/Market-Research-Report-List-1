<p><p>Pressure sealers are machines used to automate the sealing of documents, such as invoices, checks, and mailers, without the need for envelopes. The market for pressure sealers can be classified into three types. Low volume pressure sealers are compact and suitable for small businesses with lower mail volumes. Hand-feed pressure sealers are manual machines that require operators to manually feed and seal documents. Other market types include high-volume pressure sealers designed for large-scale mailings, multifunctional pressure sealers that not only seal but also fold and/or print on documents, and specialized pressure sealers for unique applications, such as self-mailers or double-window envelopes.</p></p>
