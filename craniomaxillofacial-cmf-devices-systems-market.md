<p><p>The Craniomaxillofacial (CMF) devices and systems market consists of various types of medical devices used for the treatment of maxillofacial injuries and disorders. Temporomandibular joint replacement devices are used to replace damaged joints in the jaw. Thoracic fixation devices stabilize the chest bones. Bone graft substitutes are used to replace damaged or missing bone tissue. Lastly, MF plate and screw fixation devices are used for securing fractured facial bones.</p></p>
