<p><p>CMP (Chemical Mechanical Planarization) slurry is used in the semiconductor industry for polishing and planarizing surfaces during the manufacturing process. It is available in different market types, including Alumina Slurry, Colloidal Silica Slurry, and Ceria Slurries. Alumina Slurry contains fine particles of aluminum oxide, which provides high material removal rates and excellent surface finishes. Colloidal Silica Slurry utilizes suspended particles of silica, offering precise surface planarization and low defectivity. Ceria Slurries consist of cerium oxide particles and are used for polishing advanced metals and dielectric materials, providing superior planarization and a high removal rate.</p></p>
