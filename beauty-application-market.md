<p><p>The beauty application market consists of two main types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based beauty applications operate on remote servers, allowing users to access them from anywhere with an internet connection. These applications store data on the cloud and offer features such as scalability and flexibility. On the other hand, on-premise beauty applications are installed and operated on local servers or computers. They require physical presence at the location and provide more control over data and security, but lack the convenience of accessibility from any device or location.</p></p>
