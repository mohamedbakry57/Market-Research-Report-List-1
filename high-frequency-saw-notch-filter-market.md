<p><p>High frequency surface acoustic wave (SAW) notch filters are used to attenuate unwanted frequencies in electronic devices. There are two main types of SAW notch filters in the market. Tunable SAW notch filters allow users to adjust the frequency at which the notch occurs, offering flexibility for various applications. On the other hand, non-tunable SAW notch filters have a fixed notch frequency and are typically more cost-effective.
