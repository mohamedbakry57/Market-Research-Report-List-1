<p><p>The Blast Chillers market consists of three types: Self-Contained Models, Roll-In Remote Condensing Unit Models, and Roll-Thru Remote Condensing Unit Models. Self-Contained Models are equipped with built-in condensing units, making them standalone units that can be easily moved around. Roll-In Remote Condensing Unit Models require a separate condensing unit located outside the chiller and are designed for accommodating roll-in racks. Roll-Thru Remote Condensing Unit Models also require a separate condensing unit and are designed for accommodating roll-thru racks, allowing for easy loading and unloading of products.</p></p>
