<p><p>Domestic heat recovery systems include three main types: ceiling-mount, wall-mount, and cabinet-mount. Ceiling-mount systems are installed in the ceiling of a room and are ideal for properties with limited wall space. Wall-mount systems are fixed on the wall and are suitable for rooms where ceilings cannot accommodate ventilation units. Cabinet-mount systems, on the other hand, are built into kitchen or bathroom cabinets for discreet installation. Each type offers different installation options to meet the diverse needs of homeowners and maximize heat recovery efficiency in domestic settings.</p></p>
