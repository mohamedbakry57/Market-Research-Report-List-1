<p><p>The medical sensing electrode market consists of two main types: disposable electrodes and reusable electrodes. Disposable electrodes are single-use devices that are discarded after a single patient use. They typically offer convenience and reduce the risk of cross-contamination. On the other hand, reusable electrodes are designed for multiple uses after sterilization. They are more durable, cost-effective in the long run, and commonly used in clinical settings where sterilization processes can be implemented. The choice between these types depends on factors such as cost, convenience, and infection control.</p></p>
