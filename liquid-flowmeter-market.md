<p><p>The liquid flowmeter market can be categorized into two types: stationary and portable. Stationary liquid flow meters are permanently installed in a specific location and are commonly used for industrial applications where a continuous measurement of liquid flow is required. On the other hand, portable liquid flow meters are designed for on-the-go use and can be easily transported and used in various locations. They are typically used for temporary measurements or in situations where a fixed flowmeter is not feasible or practical.</p></p>
