<p><p>Filtration sterilizers are devices used to sterilize liquids by removing microorganisms and particles. Traditional sterilizers are large, floor-standing machines commonly found in hospitals and laboratories. They use heat and pressure to kill bacteria and other pathogens. On the other hand, tabletop sterilizers are smaller, portable devices that are suitable for small-scale sterilization needs, such as in dental clinics or beauty salons. They usually operate using steam or chemical sterilization methods. Both types of sterilizers are essential for maintaining cleanliness and preventing the spread of infections in various industries.</p></p>
