<p><p>The vacuum poppet valves market consists of three main types: angle valve, Z-inline valve, and Y-inline valve. Angle valves are designed to provide 90-degree airflow direction changes, making them suitable for tight spaces. Z-inline valves allow for inline connection in zigzag patterns, enabling efficient gas flow control. Y-inline valves feature a Y-shaped configuration, allowing simultaneous control of two gas flows. These valves find applications in various industries such as semiconductor manufacturing, chemical processing, and pharmaceuticals, where accurate and precise gas flow control is required.</p></p>
