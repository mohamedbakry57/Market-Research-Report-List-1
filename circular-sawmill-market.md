<p><p>Circular sawmills are classified into two main types: movable and non-movable. Movable circular sawmills, also known as portable sawmills, are designed to be easily transported and set up at various locations. They can be towed on a trailer or carried by a truck, providing flexibility and convenience for loggers who need to work in different areas. On the other hand, non-movable circular sawmills are permanently installed at a fixed location, such as a sawmill facility, where logs are brought to be processed.</p></p>
