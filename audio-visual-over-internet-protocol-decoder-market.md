<p><p>An Audio-Visual over Internet Protocol (AVoIP) decoder is a device that enables the transmission and reception of audio and video data over an IP network. It receives compressed audio and video streams, decodes them, and then converts them back into a format that can be displayed on a monitor or played through speakers. AVoIP decoders are widely used in applications such as video conferencing, digital signage, and remote monitoring.</p><p>The AVoIP decoder market is expected to experience substantial growth in the coming years. The increasing adoption of AVoIP solutions in various industries, such as healthcare, education, and entertainment, is driving market growth. AVoIP solutions offer benefits such as increased flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness compared to traditional AV systems. Additionally, the growing demand for high-quality audio and video content and the trend towards digitalization are further fueling market growth.</p><p>The market is witnessing several trends that are shaping its growth trajectory. One such trend is the integration of AVoIP decoders with other technologies, such as artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML). This integration enables features like automatic content recognition, intelligent video analytics, and personalized user experiences.</p><p>Another trend in the AVoIP decoder market is the increasing use of IP-based networks for AV distribution. Traditional AV systems typically relied on dedicated cabling infrastructure, whereas AVoIP solutions leverage existing IP networks, reducing installation and maintenance costs.</p><p>Overall, the AVoIP decoder market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.1% during the forecast period.
