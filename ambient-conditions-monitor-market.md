<p><p>The ambient conditions monitor market includes various types of devices used to measure and monitor temperature and humidity levels in the surrounding environment. Digital thermometers are electronic devices that measure temperature accurately and display the reading digitally. Hygrometers, on the other hand, are instruments that measure humidity levels in the air. Finally, hygro-thermometers combine the capabilities of both digital thermometers and hygrometers to provide measurements of both temperature and humidity simultaneously. These devices are essential in various industries like healthcare, agriculture, and manufacturing to ensure optimal environmental conditions.</p></p>
