<p><p>Spectacle flanges, also known as spectacle blind flanges, come in different types such as spacer flanges, spade flanges, and spectacle blind flanges. Spacer flanges are used to separate or align pipes, while spade flanges have a solid plate inserted between the two flanges to completely block the flow. Spectacle blind flanges have a removable plate that allows for the flow to be completely blocked or opened. These different types of spectacle flanges serve different purposes in the piping system, ensuring flexibility and efficient flow control.</p></p>
