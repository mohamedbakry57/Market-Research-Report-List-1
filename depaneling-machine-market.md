Furthermore, the development of depaneling machines that can handle various types of PCB materials, including flexible and rigid-flex boards, is gaining traction in the market.</p><p>The market is also witnessing the introduction of portable and benchtop depaneling machines, which are compact in size and offer ease of use. <p><strong>The Depaneling Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In-line Depaneling Machine</li><li>Off-line Depaneling Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In-line Depaneling Machine is a type of machine used in manufacturing processes where the cutting of circuit boards or panels is done directly during the assembly line production. This type of machine is considered efficient and time-saving as it eliminates the need for separate processes. On the other hand, Off-line Depaneling Machine refers to a separate machine used for the cutting or separation of circuit boards or panels after they have been produced. This type of machine is used when the depaneling process is not integrated into the overall assembly line production.</p></p>
