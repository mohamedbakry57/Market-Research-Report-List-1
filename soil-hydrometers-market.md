<p><p>Soil hydrometers are devices used to measure the specific gravity of soil and sand. They are commonly used in soil testing laboratories and field inspections to determine the compaction and moisture content of soil. A soil hydrometer consists of a long glass tube with a weighted bulb at the bottom and a calibrated scale. By placing the hydrometer in a soil suspension and measuring the floatation level, the specific gravity of the soil can be determined.</p><p>The future outlook of the soil hydrometers market is promising.
