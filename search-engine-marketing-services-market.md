<p><p>Search Engine Marketing Services market types can be categorized into various pricing models. CPT (Cost Per Time) involves charging advertisers based on the duration their ads are displayed on search engine platforms. CPA (Cost-Per-Acquisition) charges advertisers only when a specific action, like a purchase or sign-up, is completed through their ad. CPC (Cost-Per-Click) charges advertisers for each click their ad receives. CPM (Cost-Per-Thousand-Impressions) charges advertisers for every thousand times their ad is displayed. Besides these, there may be other unique market types with specific pricing models utilized in Search Engine Marketing Services.</p></p>
