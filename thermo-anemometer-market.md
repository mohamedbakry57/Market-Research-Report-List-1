<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Thermo Anemometer market is divided into three types: Constant Current Anemometer, Constant Pressure Anemometer, and Thermostatic Anemometer. Constant Current Anemometers maintain a constant current through the sensing element, providing accurate measurements of air velocity. Constant Pressure Anemometers maintain a constant pressure difference across the sensing element, ensuring precise readings. Thermostatic Anemometers use a heated probe that cools down as the air flow increases, enabling the calculation of air velocity. These market types cater to different measurement needs and offer reliable and accurate airflow measurements in various applications.</p></p>
