<p><strong>The OLAP Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ROLAP</li><li>MOLAP</li><li>HMOLAP</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>OLAP (Online Analytical Processing) systems are used for analyzing large amounts of data. They have different market types. ROLAP (Relational OLAP) systems retrieve data from relational databases and perform analysis on it. MOLAP (Multidimensional OLAP) systems store data in a specialized multidimensional cube structure for efficient analysis. HMOLAP (Hybrid MOLAP) systems combine the benefits of ROLAP and MOLAP by storing summarized data in a cube while detailed data remains in a relational database. Other market types include HOLAP (Hybrid OLAP) that combines MOLAP and ROLAP in a flexible manner and WOLAP (Web-based OLAP) systems accessed through web browsers.</p></p>
