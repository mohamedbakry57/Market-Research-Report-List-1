<p><p>Bonded abrasive market can be classified into two main types: natural abrasives and synthetic abrasives. Natural abrasives are derived from naturally occurring minerals such as diamond, garnet, and corundum. These abrasives are known for their hardness and durability. Synthetic abrasives are produced in laboratories through chemical processes and include materials like silicon carbide and aluminum oxide. These abrasives are often used for their consistent performance and cost-effectiveness. Both natural and synthetic abrasives play a crucial role in various industries such as automotive, construction, and metal fabrication.</p></p>
