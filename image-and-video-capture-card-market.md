<p><p>The Image and Video Capture Card market comprises two major types: PCI/PCIe Capture Cards and USB Capture Cards. PCI/PCIe Capture Cards are designed to be inserted into the PCI or PCIe slots of a computer motherboard, providing high-speed and direct data transfer for capturing images and videos. On the other hand, USB Capture Cards offer a more convenient and accessible solution by connecting to the computer via USB ports, allowing users to easily capture and transfer images and videos without needing to install the card internally.</p></p>
