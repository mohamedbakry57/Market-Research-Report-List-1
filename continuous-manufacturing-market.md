Integrated systems combine various processes into one continuous system, while semi-continuous systems involve certain processes being carried out in batches. Other types of continuous equipment include granulators, coaters, blenders, dryers, and compressors, which perform their respective functions continuously. Semi-continuous systems for milling equipment and weighing/measurement equipment involve these processes being carried out periodically rather than continuously. These various types of continuous manufacturing equipment contribute to increased productivity and uniformity in production processes.</p></p>
