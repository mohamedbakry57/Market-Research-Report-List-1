<p><p>The Chlorine Dioxide Sensor market can be divided into two types: Analog Chlorine Dioxide Sensor and Digital Chlorine Dioxide Sensor. Analog Chlorine Dioxide Sensors measure and provide an output signal that represents the concentration of chlorine dioxide gas. They are commonly used in industrial applications where continuous measurements are required. On the other hand, Digital Chlorine Dioxide Sensors convert the measured chlorine dioxide concentration into a digital signal that can be analyzed and used for monitoring purposes. These sensors offer more advanced features and are often used in research and laboratory settings.</p></p>
