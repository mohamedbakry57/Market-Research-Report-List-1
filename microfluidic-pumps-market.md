<p><strong>The Microfluidic Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Syringe Pump</li><li>Peristaltic Pump</li><li>Pressure Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microfluidic pumps market consists of three primary types: syringe pumps, peristaltic pumps, and pressure pumps. Syringe pumps work by pushing liquid through a syringe using a plunger, allowing for precise control of fluid flow. Peristaltic pumps utilize a rotating roller mechanism to squeeze and push fluid through a tubing, commonly used in medical and laboratory applications. Pressure pumps rely on pressurized air or gas to create a flow, regulating fluid movement within microfluidic systems. These pump types are essential in various industries where accurate and controlled fluid delivery is required.</p></p>
