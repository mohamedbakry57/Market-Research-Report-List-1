<p><p>Aircraft tilt sensors are used to measure the angle of an aircraft relative to the horizon. There are two main types of tilt sensors in the market: analog type and digital type. Analog tilt sensors provide a continuous output signal that varies with the angle of tilt, while digital tilt sensors provide a discrete output signal that indicates whether the aircraft is tilted beyond a certain threshold. Both types have their own applications and advantages in the aviation industry, catering to different needs and requirements of aircraft manufacturers and operators.</p></p>
