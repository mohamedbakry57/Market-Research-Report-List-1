<p><strong>The Mammography Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Analog Mammography Systems</li><li>Digital Mammography Systems</li><li>Breast Tomosynthesis Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mammography systems are classified into three types: analog mammography systems, digital mammography systems, and breast tomosynthesis systems.</p><p>Analog mammography systems use traditional X-ray films to capture breast images. Digital mammography systems employ electronic detectors to produce digital images that can be stored and analyzed electronically. Breast tomosynthesis systems create a series of detailed 3D images of the breast by taking multiple low-dose X-rays from different angles. These systems offer increased accuracy in detecting breast abnormalities, aiding in early diagnosis and improved treatment outcomes.</p></p>
