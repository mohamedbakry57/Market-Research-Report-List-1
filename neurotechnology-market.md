<p><p>The Neurotechnology market can be divided into several types. Imaging Modalities include techniques like MRI and fMRI, which provide detailed images of brain structure and activity. Neurostimulation involves using electrical or magnetic impulses to stimulate specific brain areas, for applications like deep brain stimulation or transcranial magnetic stimulation. Cranial Surface Measurement technologies assess the electrical activity of the brain through the scalp, utilizing devices like EEG or MEG. Neurological Implants involve the use of surgically implanted devices for various purposes such as deep brain stimulation or cochlear implants. Lastly, Other technologies include brain-computer interfaces and neurofeedback devices.</p></p>
