<p><p>Structured cabling systems market consists of various types, including Copper Wire, Optical Fiber, and Other. Copper Wire refers to the use of copper cables to transmit data over short distances. Optical Fiber, on the other hand, utilizes thin strands of glass or plastic to transmit data as pulses of light over long distances, providing high-speed and long-range connectivity. The Other category encompasses alternative cabling solutions such as coaxial cables or wireless connections. Each market type offers distinct advantages and is used based on specific requirements and infrastructure limitations.</p></p>
