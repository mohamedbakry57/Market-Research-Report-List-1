<p><strong>The Sleep Therapy Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CPAP (Continuous Positive Airway Pressure)</li><li>BiPAP (Bilevel Positive Airway Pressure)</li><li>VPAP (Variable Positive Airway Pressure)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sleep therapy devices are used to treat sleep-related disorders such as sleep apnea. CPAP machines deliver a constant and continuous air pressure to keep the airways open during sleep. BiPAP machines provide two different air pressure levels, higher during inhalation and lower during exhalation. VPAP machines offer variable air pressure based on the individual's breathing patterns. These devices help to maintain adequate airflow and promote uninterrupted sleep, thereby improving overall sleep quality and reducing symptoms of sleep disorders.</p></p>
