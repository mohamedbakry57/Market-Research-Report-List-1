<p><p>Chemiluminescence reagent market types include indirect chemiluminescence, direct chemiluminescence, and electrochemiluminescence. In indirect chemiluminescence, a secondary reagent reacts with a labeled molecule, producing light. Direct chemiluminescence involves the reaction of a labeled molecule directly with a substrate, resulting in light emission. Electrochemiluminescence utilizes an electric field to generate light through a photoactive compound. These different types of chemiluminescence reagents have various applications in fields such as immunoassays, clinical diagnostics, forensic analysis, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
