<p><ul><li>Permanent Magnet</li><li>Superconductive Magnet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MRI magnet shimming coil market is categorized into two types: Permanent Magnet and Superconductive Magnet. Permanent Magnet coils are constructed using a fixed magnetic field strength. These coils are cost-effective and require minimal maintenance. On the other hand, Superconductive Magnet coils use electrical current to create a powerful magnetic field. These coils offer high image quality but are expensive and require cryogenic cooling systems. Both types of coils play a crucial role in ensuring accurate and detailed imaging in MRI scanners.</p></p>
