<p><strong>The PO Hot Melt Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Amorphous Polyolefin Hot Melt</li><li>Crystal Polyolefin Hot Melt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PO Hot Melt Adhesives are categorized into two main types: Amorphous Polyolefin Hot Melt and Crystal Polyolefin Hot Melt. Amorphous Polyolefin Hot Melt adhesives have a non-crystalline structure, making them flexible, transparent, and resistant to UV radiation. On the other hand, Crystal Polyolefin Hot Melt adhesives have a crystalline structure, making them harder and more durable. Both types offer strong bonding capabilities and are commonly used in industries such as packaging, woodworking, and automotive.</p></p>
