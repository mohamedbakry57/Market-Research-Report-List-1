<p><p>Anesthesia gas mixers are devices used in medical settings to blend different gases for the delivery of anesthesia during surgical procedures. There are two types of anesthesia gas mixers: manual and automatic. Manual mixers require the user to manually adjust the gas flow rates and concentrations by rotating knobs or adjusting valves. On the other hand, automatic mixers utilize electronic controls to regulate gas flow rates and maintain the desired gas concentrations. Automatic mixers offer more precise control and are often preferred in modern healthcare settings.</p></p>
