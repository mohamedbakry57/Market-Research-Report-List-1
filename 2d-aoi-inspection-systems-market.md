<p><strong>The 2D AOI Inspection Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inline 2D AOI</li><li>Offline 2D AOI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inline 2D AOI refers to the type of system that is integrated into the production line, allowing for real-time inspection of printed circuit boards (PCBs) during the manufacturing process. It provides immediate feedback and helps in identifying any defects or anomalies to ensure the quality of the finished product. On the other hand, Offline 2D AOI systems are separate from the production line and are typically used for post-production inspection. They are useful for analyzing and improving the overall manufacturing process by providing detailed reports and analysis of defects found on the PCBs.</p></p>
