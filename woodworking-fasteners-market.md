<p><strong>The Woodworking Fasteners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Threaded Fasteners</li><li>Non-threaded Fasteners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Woodworking Fasteners Market comprises two main types: Threaded Fasteners and Non-threaded Fasteners. Threaded Fasteners are screws, bolts, and studs that have spiraled threads to securely fasten wood pieces together. They offer high tensile strength and are commonly used in furniture, cabinets, and construction. On the other hand, Non-threaded Fasteners include nails, staples, and dowels that provide quick and easy connection between wood components. They are often used for temporary or aesthetic purposes, such as attaching trim, molding, or decorative elements to wood surfaces.</p></p>
