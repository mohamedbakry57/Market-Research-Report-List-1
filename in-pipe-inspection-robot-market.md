<p><p>Wheel In-pipe Inspection Robots are designed with wheels to navigate through pipes and inspect for any damages or blockages. They are best suited for straight and smooth pipes. Tracked In-pipe Inspection Robots, on the other hand, use tracks for movement, allowing them to traverse through pipes with rough or uneven surfaces. They provide better stability and maneuverability in challenging pipe conditions. Both types of robots offer efficient and accurate inspections of pipelines, helping to prevent potential issues and ensure proper maintenance.</p></p>
