<p><p>The baby canned supplements market consists of three main types: puree (paste) canned food, granular canned food, and juice canned food. Puree canned food is made by blending fruits or vegetables into a smooth paste, offering a convenient way to introduce solid food to babies. Granular canned food is made by finely grinding ingredients into small particles, providing a texture suitable for older infants. Juice canned food involves extracting liquid from fruits or vegetables, offering a tasty and nutritious beverage for babies. These different types cater to the varying needs and preferences of parents and their babies.</p></p>
