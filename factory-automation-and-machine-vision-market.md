<p><p>Factory automation refers to the use of computer-controlled machinery and systems to automate manufacturing processes. Machine vision, on the other hand, involves the use of cameras and image processing software to inspect, analyze, and interpret visual information in real-time.</p><p>The Factory Automation and Machine Vision Market is experiencing significant growth and is expected to continue this trend in the coming years.
