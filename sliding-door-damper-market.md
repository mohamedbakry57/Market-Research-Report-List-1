<p><strong>The Sliding Door Damper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Damper</li><li>Pneumatic Damper</li><li>Hydraulic Damper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sliding door damper market comprises three main types of dampers: mechanical dampers, pneumatic dampers, and hydraulic dampers. Mechanical dampers function by using mechanical components, such as springs or counterweights, to control the door's speed and movement. Pneumatic dampers, on the other hand, use compressed air to regulate the door's motion, providing smooth and controlled operation. Hydraulic dampers rely on a hydraulic fluid to dampen the door's movement, resulting in a quieter and safer operation. Each type of damper offers unique features and advantages for various sliding door applications.</p></p>
