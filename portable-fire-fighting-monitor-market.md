<p><strong>The Portable Fire Fighting Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Inlet</li><li>Twin Inlet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Portable Fire Fighting Monitor market includes two types: Single Inlet and Twin Inlet. Single Inlet monitors consist of a single water inlet that allows the firefighter to direct the water flow in a specific direction. This type is typically lightweight and portable, making it suitable for smaller fires. On the other hand, Twin Inlet monitors have two water inlets, enabling the user to simultaneously control two water flows. This type is more powerful and generally used for larger fires or situations that require a higher water output.</p></p>
