<p><strong>The Bioabsorbable Surgical Implant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Synthetic Material</li><li>Natural Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bioabsorbable surgical implant market comprises two major types: synthetic material and natural material. Synthetic materials are man-made substances designed to mimic the properties of natural tissues. They are often derived from polymers such as polyglycolic acid, polylactic acid, and polycaprolactone. On the other hand, natural materials are derived from organic sources and include materials like collagen, chitosan, and silk. Both types of implants are designed to degrade and be absorbed by the body over time, avoiding the need for subsequent surgical removal.</p></p>
