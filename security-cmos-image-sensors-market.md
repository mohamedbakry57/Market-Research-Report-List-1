<p><p>Security CMOS (Complementary Metal Oxide Semiconductor) image sensors are electronic devices that capture images and convert them into electrical signals. Several factors contribute to this growth, including the increasing demand for security cameras and surveillance systems for residential, commercial, and industrial applications. The advancements in technology, such as the integration of CMOS image sensors with artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning capabilities, further drive the market growth.</p><p>The demand for high-resolution images, low power consumption, and compact size in security cameras is also propelling the market growth. The emergence of the Internet of Things (IoT) and smart city initiatives is expected to create lucrative opportunities for security CMOS image sensors in the market.</p><p>In addition, the increasing need for enhanced security measures, such as facial recognition and video analytics, is further fueling the demand for security CMOS image sensors. These sensors enable real-time monitoring and analysis of video footage, thereby improving overall security efficiency.</p><p>The market is witnessing several trends, such as the integration of CMOS image sensors with infrared (IR) technology for night vision capabilities and the development of sensors with high dynamic range (HDR) for capturing images in varying lighting conditions. Other trends include the adoption of back-illuminated CMOS image sensors for improved sensitivity and the use of global shutter technology to eliminate motion distortion in fast-moving objects.</p><p>Overall, the global Security CMOS Image Sensors market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.9% during the forecast period, driven by the increasing demand for security systems, technological advancements, and emerging trends in the market.</p></p>
