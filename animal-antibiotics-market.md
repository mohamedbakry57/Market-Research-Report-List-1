<p><p>The animal antibiotics market can be categorized into various types. Tetracyclines, including chlortetracycline and oxytetracycline, are widely used to treat respiratory and gastrointestinal infections. Penicillins, such as amoxicillin and ampicillin, are effective against a broad range of bacterial infections. Sulfonamides, like sulfadiazine and sulfamethoxazole, work by inhibiting bacterial growth. Macrolides, including erythromycin and tylosin, are used for respiratory and enteric infections. Aminoglycosides, like streptomycin and gentamicin, are effective against severe bacterial infections. Cephalosporins, such as ceftiofur and cefalexin, have a broad spectrum of activity and are commonly used in veterinary medicine. Lastly, there are other types of antibiotics used in animals to combat different bacterial infections.</p></p>
