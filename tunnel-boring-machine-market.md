<p><p>The Tunnel Boring Machine (TBM) market consists of two main types: Soft Ground TBMs and Hard Rock TBMs. Soft Ground TBMs are designed to excavate tunnels through soil, clay, and other soft materials. They often utilize a pressurized face shield to prevent cave-ins. On the other hand, Hard Rock TBMs are specifically designed for excavating through solid rock formations. They use disc cutters and other powerful tools to break and remove the rock. Both types of TBMs play a crucial role in various construction and infrastructure projects worldwide.</p></p>
