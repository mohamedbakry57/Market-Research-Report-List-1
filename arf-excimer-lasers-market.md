<p><p>The ArF Excimer Lasers market consists of two types: ArF Dry Light Sources and ArF Immersion Light Sources. ArF Dry Light Sources employ gas as the medium to generate ultra-violet (UV) light, which is then used for various applications such as lithography in the semiconductor industry. On the other hand, ArF Immersion Light Sources use a liquid immersion medium to enhance the performance of lithography machines, allowing for higher resolution and improved processing capabilities. Both types play a significant role in enabling advanced manufacturing processes in various industries.</p></p>
