<p><p>The Orogastric Tube market consists of three major types: flexible tube, rigid tube, and semi-rigid tube. </p><p>Flexible tubes are made of soft and pliable materials, allowing for easy insertion and manipulation within the gastrointestinal tract. Rigid tubes, on the other hand, are made of stiff materials and maintain their shape during insertion. Semi-rigid tubes have a partially flexible design, providing a balance between the benefits of both flexible and rigid tubes. The choice of tube type depends on the patient's condition and the desired application.</p></p>
