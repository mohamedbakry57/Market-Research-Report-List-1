Membrane separators utilize semi-permeable membranes to separate different components of a liquid mixture. Evaporators are used to separate liquids from solids by vaporizing the liquid, leaving behind the solid. The "others" category includes various types of separators, such as sieves and filters, which are utilized based on specific industry requirements.</p></p>
