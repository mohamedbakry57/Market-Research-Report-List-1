<p><ul><li>Soft Armor</li><li>Hard Armor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bullet-resistant vest market is divided into two types: soft armor and hard armor. Soft armor vests are made from flexible materials like Kevlar or Spectra, providing comfort and mobility. They can effectively protect against lower-velocity bullets and sharp-edged weapons. On the other hand, hard armor vests are constructed with rigid plates made of ceramic or metal, offering enhanced protection against high-velocity bullets and rifle rounds. Despite being heavier and less flexible, hard armor provides greater defense against severe threats, suitable for military or tactical operations.</p></p>
