<p><p>The Land Mobile Radio (LMR) market consists of three main types: VHF (Very High Frequency), UHF (Ultra High Frequency), and SHF (Super High Frequency). VHF operates within the frequency ranges of 40MHz to 174MHz, UHF operates within the range of 200MHz to 512MHz, and SHF operates within the range of 700MHz to 1000MHz. These frequency bands determine the radio's capabilities in terms of range and signal penetration. Each type caters to different communication needs in various industries such as public safety, transportation, and construction.</p></p>
