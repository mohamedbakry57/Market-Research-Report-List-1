<p><p>The Outdoor Lightning Arrester Market comprises several types of lightning arresters, including Rod Lightning Arrester, Horn Gap Lightning Arrester, and Multi Gap Lightning Arrester. Rod Lightning Arrester is commonly used and consists of a metal rod placed atop a structure to intercept lightning strikes and redirect the electrical current into the ground. Horn Gap Lightning Arrester operates by creating a gap between two electrodes, allowing the current to arc across and dissipate. Multi Gap Lightning Arrester employs multiple gaps to increase the voltage withstanding capacity and protect against lightning-induced surges. These lightning arresters play a crucial role in safeguarding structures from lightning-related damage.</p></p>
