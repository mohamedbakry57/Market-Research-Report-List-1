<p><p>The ambient lighting market encompasses several types of lighting options. Recessed lights are installed into the ceiling for a sleek and seamless appearance. Surface mounted lights are attached to the surface of a wall or ceiling, providing direct illumination. Suspended lights are hung from the ceiling, creating a striking visual effect. Track lights are adjustable and can be mounted on tracks to highlight specific areas. Strip lights are flexible and can be easily installed on any surface, offering a continuous glow. Each type of lighting caters to different preferences and requirements in ambient illumination.</p></p>
