<p><p>Waterless urinals are an innovative solution for conserving water in bathroom facilities. The market for waterless urinals consists of various types, including liquid sealant cartridges, membrane traps, biological blocks, and mechanical balls. Liquid sealant cartridges use a liquid substance to trap odors and prevent the escape of gases, while membrane traps utilize a flexible membrane to seal off the drain. Biological blocks contain enzymes that break down waste, and mechanical balls use a ball mechanism to form a barrier. These different types provide alternatives to traditional water-flushing urinals, promoting water conservation and reducing maintenance needs.</p></p>
