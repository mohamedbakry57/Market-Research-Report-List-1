<p><strong>The Automatic Screen Changers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Piston</li><li>Double Piston</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic screen changers are devices used in polymer processing to replace dirty screens with clean screens without interrupting the production process. Single piston screen changers use a single piston to move the screen between dirty and clean positions, suitable for applications with low to medium pressure requirements. On the other hand, double piston screen changers employ two pistons for faster and more efficient screen changing, making them ideal for high-pressure applications. Both types improve productivity by minimizing downtime and ensuring continuous production.</p></p>
