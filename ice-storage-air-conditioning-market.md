<p><strong>The Ice Storage Air Conditioning Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Ice Making</li><li>Dynamic Ice Making</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ice storage air conditioning refers to a system that uses ice as a thermal storage medium to cool indoor spaces. There are two types of ice making methods utilized in this market. Static ice making involves the production of ice during off-peak hours using chillers, which is then stored and used for cooling during peak demand periods. Dynamic ice making, on the other hand, involves the continuous production of ice throughout the day using chillers and ice-on-coil units. Both methods aim to reduce energy consumption and optimize cooling efficiency in air conditioning systems.</p></p>
