<p><p>Sport sunglasses come in two main types: polarized and non-polarized. Polarized sunglasses have lenses that reduce glare caused by reflections from flat surfaces like water or snow. They provide clearer vision and better eye protection, making them ideal for outdoor activities like watersports or skiing. Non-polarized sunglasses, on the other hand, do not have this glare-reducing feature. They are still effective in protecting the eyes from harmful UV rays, but may not provide the same clarity and visual comfort as polarized sunglasses.</p></p>
