<p><strong>The Automatic Pool Cleaning Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Suction-side Pool Cleaners</li><li>Pressure-side Pool Cleaners</li><li>Electric Pool Cleaners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic pool cleaning machines market consists of three main types: suction-side pool cleaners, pressure-side pool cleaners, and electric pool cleaners. Suction-side pool cleaners utilize the pool's filtration system to suck up debris and dirt. Pressure-side pool cleaners operate by using the water pressure generated by the pool's pump to move debris into a filter bag. Electric pool cleaners are powered by electricity and use brushes, scrubbers, and suction to clean the pool surfaces. These different types offer various advantages and cater to different pool cleaning needs.</p></p>
