<p><strong>The ASIC Design Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standard Cell Based ASIC</li><li>Gate Array Based ASIC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ASIC design service market consists of two major types: Standard Cell Based ASIC and Gate Array Based ASIC. In Standard Cell Based ASIC, designers create custom circuits by using pre-designed standard cells, resulting in higher performance and lower power consumption. Gate Array Based ASIC, on the other hand, involves using a pre-fabricated chip with a generic array of basic gates that can be programmed to create custom designs. This approach offers faster turnaround time but may result in lower performance and higher power consumption compared to Standard Cell Based ASIC.</p></p>
