<p><p>The Solar Street Light Controller market is divided into two types: MPPT Controller and PWM Controller. MPPT (Maximum Power Point Tracking) Controller is an advanced technology that optimizes the power output from solar panels by tracking the maximum power point. It increases the system efficiency and is suitable for areas with unstable sunlight. On the other hand, PWM (Pulse Width Modulation) Controller is a basic and cost-effective controller that adjusts the charging and discharging of batteries. It is suitable for stable sunlight areas and has simple operation and maintenance.</p></p>
