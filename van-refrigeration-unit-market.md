<p><p>In the van refrigeration unit market, there are two types: vehicle powered and self-powered. Vehicle powered refers to refrigeration units that are powered by the vehicle's engine or battery, using the vehicle's fuel or electrical system. These units are ideal for short distances and frequent stops. On the other hand, self-powered units have their own power sources, such as small engines or electric motors, enabling them to operate independently of the vehicle. These units are more commonly used for long-distance transportation or when the vehicle is not running.</p></p>
