<p><strong>The Parachute Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Round</li><li>Cruciform</li><li>Annular and pull down apex</li><li>Rogallo wing</li><li>Ribbon and Ring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Parachute markets can be categorized into different types based on their designs and shapes. Round parachutes are the most traditional and commonly used type, providing stability and easy deployment. Cruciform parachutes have a cross-shaped canopy for better control and maneuverability. Annular and pull-down apex parachutes feature a circular canopy for improved aerodynamics and precision landing. Rogallo wing parachutes have a delta-shaped design for increased glide ratio. Ribbon and ring parachutes have a unique structure with ribbons and rings for stability and control during descent.</p></p>
