<p><p>The cryogenic check valves market includes different types such as manual, electric, pneumatic, and hydraulic valves. Manual check valves are operated by hand, allowing for manual control of the valve's opening and closing. Electric check valves are controlled by an electric motor, which provides automated functionality. Pneumatic check valves use compressed air to actuate the valve, enabling quick and efficient operation. Hydraulic check valves, on the other hand, utilize hydraulic pressure to open or close, ensuring reliable and precise control. Each type of valve offers unique features and benefits to cater to specific industry needs.</p></p>
