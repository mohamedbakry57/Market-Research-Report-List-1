<p><strong>The GFRP Rebars Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Smooth GFRB Rebars</li><li>Sand Coated GFRP Rebars</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GFRP (Glass Fiber Reinforced Polymer) Rebars market can be divided into two types: Smooth GFRP Rebars and Sand Coated GFRP Rebars. Smooth GFRP Rebars are made of glass fibers embedded in a polymer matrix, resulting in a smooth surface. These rebars are generally used in applications where high corrosion resistance is required, such as in marine structures. On the other hand, Sand Coated GFRP Rebars have a layer of sand particles on their surface, which enhances their bond strength with concrete. These rebars are commonly used in construction projects for their improved adhesion properties.</p></p>
