<p><p>The chocolate powdered drinks market consists of various types, including freeze dried, spray dried, and chicory based products. Freeze dried chocolate drinks are made by freezing the liquid form and then removing the water through sublimation. Spray dried chocolate drinks are created by spraying hot liquid chocolate into a chamber where it quickly dries into powdered form. Chicory based chocolate drinks are made by adding chicory root extract to the chocolate powder, providing a unique flavor profile. These different methods offer consumers a variety of options when choosing their preferred chocolate powdered drink.</p></p>
