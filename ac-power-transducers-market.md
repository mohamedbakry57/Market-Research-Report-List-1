Single Phase transducers are capable of measuring power in single-phase electrical systems. Dual Phase transducers are designed for two-phase electrical systems, while Multi Phase transducers are specifically made for measuring power in three-phase electrical systems. Each type of transducer caters to different power measurement requirements, allowing accurate and efficient monitoring and control of electrical systems.</p></p>
