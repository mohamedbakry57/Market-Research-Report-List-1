<p><p>The single girder gantry crane market consists of two main types: the gantry crane and the half-gantry crane. A gantry crane is a type of crane that has two upright beams and a connecting beam, forming a structure resembling a gantry. It is usually used for heavy lifting tasks in warehouses, shipyards, and construction sites. On the other hand, a half-gantry crane only has one upright beam, which is typically supported by the building structure. This type of crane is commonly used in situations where there is limited space or when one side of the lifting area is free from obstructions.</p></p>
