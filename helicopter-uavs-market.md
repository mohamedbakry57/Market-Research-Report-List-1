<p><p>The helicopter UAVs market consists of various types based on their rotor configurations. The single rotor type features a main rotor and tail rotor for stability and control. The coaxial rotor type eliminates the need for a tail rotor by utilizing two rotors stacked one above the other. The tandem rotor type includes two rotors positioned one behind the other, enhancing lifting capability. The quad-rotor type employs four rotors arranged in a square pattern, ensuring stability and maneuverability. Each rotor configuration offers unique advantages and applications in the helicopter UAVs market.</p></p>
