<p><p>The safety switch market consists of two main types: contact switch and non-contact switch. Contact switches are designed to be physically activated by direct contact, providing a simple and secure safety solution. On the other hand, non-contact switches use advanced technologies such as sensors or magnets to detect the presence or absence of objects without the need for physical contact. Both types offer crucial safety features and are widely used in various industries to prevent accidents and ensure the protection of workers and equipment.</p></p>
