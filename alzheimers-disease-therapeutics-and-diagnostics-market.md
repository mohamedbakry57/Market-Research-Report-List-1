<p><p>Alzheimer's Disease Therapeutics and Diagnostics Market comprises various types: Biomarkers, Cholinesterase inhibitors, NMDA receptor antagonists, Brain imaging, and Blood tests. Biomarkers are biological indicators used to detect the presence or progression of the disease. Cholinesterase inhibitors help manage symptoms by increasing the levels of acetylcholine in the brain. NMDA receptor antagonists prevent excessive glutamate activity in the brain. Brain imaging techniques like PET and MRI allow visualizing structural and functional changes. Blood tests help detect specific biomarkers or genetic mutations associated with Alzheimer's disease, aiding in early diagnosis and monitoring of the disease.</p></p>
