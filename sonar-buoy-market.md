<p><strong>The Sonar Buoy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Sonar Buoy</li><li>Passive Sonar Buoy</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Sonar buoys are devices used in underwater surveillance and navigation systems. The active sonar buoy emits sound waves and analyzes the reflected signals to detect objects underwater. It is primarily used for deep-sea or long-range applications. On the other hand, the passive sonar buoy relies on picking up sounds emitted by underwater objects to detect their presence. It is commonly used for short-range or coastal applications. Both types of sonar buoys play a crucial role in military, scientific research, and commercial applications, providing valuable underwater intelligence and enhancing maritime security.</p></p>
