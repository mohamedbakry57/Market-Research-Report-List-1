<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Optical Encryption Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>OTN or Layer 1</li><li>MACsec or Layer 2</li><li>IPsec or Layer 3</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical Encryption is a technology that secures data transmissions over fiber-optic networks. OTN or Layer 1 encryption focuses on encrypting the entire optical transport network at the physical layer. MACsec or Layer 2 encryption secures Ethernet frames, providing end-to-end security between network switches. IPsec or Layer 3 encryption encrypts IP packets, ensuring secure communication between network devices at the network layer. Each encryption market type addresses specific security concerns at different layers of the network, providing comprehensive protection for different types of data transmissions.</p></p>
