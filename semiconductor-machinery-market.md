<p><p>The semiconductor machinery market consists of two main types: Semiconductor Front-end Equipment and Semiconductor Back-end Equipment. Semiconductor Front-end Equipment involves the production of semiconductor devices starting from a silicon wafer, including processes such as etching, deposition, and lithography. On the other hand, Semiconductor Back-end Equipment involves the assembly and testing of individual semiconductor devices, including processes such as packaging, bonding, and electrical testing. Both types play crucial roles in the semiconductor manufacturing process, ensuring the production of high-quality and reliable semiconductor devices.</p></p>
