<p><p>Fabric ducting is a type of ventilation system that uses permeable fabric materials instead of traditional metal ducts. This innovative market is classified into two types: round fabric ducting and semi-rigid fabric ducting. Round fabric ducting is flexible and lightweight, providing even air distribution throughout a space. It is suitable for various applications, such as commercial buildings and industrial facilities. On the other hand, semi-rigid fabric ducting has internal support structures, ensuring better shape retention and higher air velocity. Both types offer energy efficiency, easy installation, and low maintenance, making fabric ducting increasingly popular in the market.</p></p>
