<p><strong>The Colorectal Cancer Diagnostics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FOB Tests</li><li>CTC Tests</li><li>CEA Tests</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Colorectal cancer diagnostics market includes various types of tests. Fecal Occult Blood (FOB) tests, also known as stool blood tests, detect small amounts of blood in stool samples. Circulating Tumor Cells (CTC) tests examine the presence of cancer cells in the blood. Carcinoembryonic Antigen (CEA) tests measure levels of a protein called CEA, which can be elevated in colorectal cancer patients. Other diagnostic methods may involve imaging techniques, genetic tests, or biopsy analysis. These tests aid in the detection and monitoring of colorectal cancer for effective treatment and management.</p></p>
