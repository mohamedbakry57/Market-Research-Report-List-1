<p><p>There are two main types of anesthesia machines: trolley-mounted and for table use. Trolley-mounted anesthesia machines are commonly found in operating rooms and are designed to be mobile, allowing easy movement within the hospital or clinic. They typically have more features and capabilities, such as multiple gas cylinders and advanced monitoring systems. On the other hand, table-mounted anesthesia machines are more compact and are primarily used in smaller medical facilities. They are fixed to the operating table, offering convenience and space-saving advantages while still providing essential anesthesia delivery functions.</p></p>
