<p><strong>The Offshore Support Vessels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Platform Supply Vessels</li><li>Multi-purpose Supply vessels</li><li>Anchor Handling Vessels</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Offshore support vessels (OSVs) are classified into various types based on their functionalities and capabilities. Platform Supply Vessels (PSVs) specialize in delivering supplies, equipment, and personnel to offshore installations. Multi-purpose Supply Vessels (MPSVs) have diverse capabilities and can perform tasks such as platform supply, subsea construction, and well intervention. Anchor Handling Vessels (AHVs) are designed for managing and maneuvering anchors for floating structures. Lastly, the "Others" category includes OSVs with unique features like seismic support vessels, remotely operated vehicle support vessels, and diving support vessels, among others.</p></p>
