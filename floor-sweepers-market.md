<p><p>Floor sweepers are cleaning machines used in various industries and settings. The market for floor sweepers consists of two types: ride-on and walk-behind sweepers. Ride-on sweepers are larger machines that allow operators to sit on the equipment while cleaning large areas efficiently. These sweepers are ideal for cleaning warehouses, parking lots, and other expansive spaces. On the other hand, walk-behind sweepers are smaller machines operated by a person walking behind the equipment. These sweepers are suitable for cleaning smaller areas or spaces that are harder to reach, such as narrow aisles or corners.</p></p>
