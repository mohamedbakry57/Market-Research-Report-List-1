<p><p>Milling tools are a vital component of machining operations, with various types catering to specific needs. Recessing milling tools are designed for creating recesses or slots in materials. Roughing milling tools are employed for removing large amounts of material quickly. Contouring milling tools are used to create complex shapes and contours. Face mills are primarily used for face milling operations, while semi-finishing cutters provide a balance between roughing and finishing. Threading milling tools are utilized for cutting threads on workpieces, enhancing their functionality and adaptability.</p></p>
