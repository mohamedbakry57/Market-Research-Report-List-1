<p><p>The optometry exam equipment market consists of various types of devices used for eye examinations. OCT (Optical Coherence Tomography) is used for detailed imaging of the structures within the eye. Fundus cameras capture images of the retina. Perimeters measure the visual field for diagnosing glaucoma. Ophthalmoscopes examine the back of the eye, while ultrasounds evaluate eye structures. Autorefractors measure refractive errors, and slit lamps examine the anterior eye segment. Corneal topography maps the cornea's surface, lensmeters measure eyeglass prescription, and chart projectors display eye charts for vision testing.</p></p>
