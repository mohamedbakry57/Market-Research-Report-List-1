Helix type sensors consist of two different metals coiled together in a helical shape, which expands and contracts with temperature changes. Cantilever type sensors use two dissimilar metals joined at one end and bend when heated. Spiral type sensors have a coiled structure similar to helix type sensors. Flat type sensors utilize two metal strips bonded together, while other types may include variations or combinations of these designs.</p></p>
