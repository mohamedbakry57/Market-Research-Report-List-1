<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Dental Scaling Units Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sonic Scalers</li><li>Magnetostrictive Scalers</li><li>Piezoelectric Scalers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrasonic dental scaling units are devices used in dentistry for plaque and tartar removal from teeth. There are three types of ultrasonic scalers: sonic scalers, magnetostrictive scalers, and piezoelectric scalers. Sonic scalers operate at a frequency of 3,000 to 8,000 Hz and create vibrations through a motor-driven mechanism. Magnetostrictive scalers use magnetic fields to generate vibrations at frequencies around 25,000 Hz. Piezoelectric scalers utilize a ceramic transducer that converts electrical energy into vibrations, typically ranging from 24,000 to 48,000 Hz. These different types provide dentists with options for effective and efficient dental cleaning.</p></p>
