The market for voltage references can be categorized into four types: precision voltage references, adjustable voltage references, shunt voltage references, and programmable voltage references. </p><p>Precision voltage references provide a high level of accuracy and stability in voltage output. Adjustable voltage references can be adjusted to generate different voltage levels, suitable for various applications. Shunt voltage references operate by adjusting the current flowing through a shunt resistor to control the voltage output. Programmable voltage references offer the flexibility to program and customize the voltage output according to specific requirements.</p></p>
