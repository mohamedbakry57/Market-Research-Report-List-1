<p><p>Metal Corrugated Compensators, also known as metal bellows, are flexible elements used in piping systems to compensate for thermal expansion, vibration, or misalignment. The ability of metal corrugated compensators to withstand high pressures and temperatures makes them suitable for applications in these industries. Additionally, the growing emphasis on safety and the need for leak-proof connections in piping systems have further boosted the market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, the increasing use of metal corrugated compensators in HVAC systems, water treatment plants, and industrial machinery is driving market growth.
