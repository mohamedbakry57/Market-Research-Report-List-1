<p><p>The antimicrobial food disinfectants market consists of three main types: liquid, granule, and spray. Liquid disinfectants are commonly used in foodservice establishments for sanitizing surfaces or utensils. Granule disinfectants are solid particles that dissolve in water to create a disinfecting solution. They are often used for large-scale cleaning and disinfection. Spray disinfectants come in liquid form and are applied as a mist onto surfaces. They are convenient for small-scale disinfection and are commonly used in household kitchens and restaurants.</p></p>
