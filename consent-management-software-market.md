<p><p>Consent Management Software (CMS) is used by organizations to manage user consent and preferences related to data collection and processing. The market for CMS can be categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premise. </p><p>1. Cloud-based CMS: In this type, the software is accessed and hosted on remote servers, allowing users to manage consent policies and preferences through a web-based interface. It offers flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness, as the software and its updates are managed by the CMS provider.</p><p>2. On-premise CMS: With this type, organizations host the software locally on their own servers. It provides more control and privacy, but requires additional resources for setup, maintenance, and updates. This option is chosen by organizations with strict data security regulations or specialized infrastructure requirements.</p></p>
