<p><p>Hose clamps are used to secure hoses in various applications. There are different types of hose clamps available in the market. Screw/Band (Worm Gear) Clamps are adjustable clamp types that use a screw mechanism to tighten the band around the hose. Spring Clamps have a coiled spring that provides constant pressure to hold the hose in place. Wire Clamps utilize a wire that is wrapped around the hose and tightened. Ear Clamps feature a band with one or two ears that are crimped to secure the hose. Other types of hose clamps might include T-bolt clamps, over center latch clamps, or constant tension clamps.</p></p>
