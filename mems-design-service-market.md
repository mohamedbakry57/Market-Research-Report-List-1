<p><strong>The MEMS Design Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Variable Capacitive Type</li><li>Piezoresistive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MEMS Design Service Market includes two major types: Variable Capacitive Type and Piezoresistive Type. In Variable Capacitive Type, the design involves the use of capacitive sensing elements to measure changes in capacitance caused by external forces, enabling detection of motion, pressure, or acceleration. On the other hand, Piezoresistive Type incorporates piezoresistive sensing elements that change resistance when subjected to external forces, allowing measurement of pressure, strain, or force. Both types offer unique design services tailored to specific applications in various industries like automotive, consumer electronics, and healthcare.</p></p>
