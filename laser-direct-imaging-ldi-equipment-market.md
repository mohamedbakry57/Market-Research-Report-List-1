<p><ul><li>Polygon Mirror 365nm</li><li>DMD 405nm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laser Direct Imaging (LDI) Equipment market consists of two main types: Polygon Mirror 365nm and DMD 405nm. Polygon Mirror 365nm systems use a rotating polygon mirror to direct the laser beam onto the substrate for high-speed imaging. On the other hand, DMD 405nm systems utilize a digital micromirror device to selectively reflect the laser light, allowing for precise and accurate imaging. Both types offer unique advantages in terms of speed, precision, and resolution for various applications in the manufacturing industry.</p></p>
